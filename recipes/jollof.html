JOLLOF RICE

DESCRIPTION

Jollof rice (NIgerian Jollof to be precise is a local dish made from rice and other spices.

It's one of the most demanding food in the country and it happens to be my best.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes (Fresh and Satchet or tin tomatoes
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Onions
  • Curry (powder and leaf)
  • Thyme
  • Any other preferable spices (just one is enough)

Steps

  1. Parboil the rice
  2. Fry the tomatoes with the spices
  3. Add the stock of your meat
  4. Pour your rice into the fried stew; do not stir to avoid getting burnt
  5. Add water
  6. Keep checking it until the rice is soft to you taste
  7. Stir
  8. Your delicious jollof rice is ready to be served
